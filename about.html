Bringing Innovation and Care to the Community

PT Abadi Perkasa Bersama Alksesindo (APBA) was established on October 3, 2017, based on the Notarial Deed of Hj. Wiwik R. Suparno SH., M.Kn - No. 6 - dated October 3, 2017. PT APBA is a company engaged in the provision of medical equipment, with a focus on the field of urology. In providing medical equipment, PT APBA carries out several business activities.

  • Vision

    To become a competent and consistent medical equipment company in Indonesia that actively participates in improving the health of the Indonesian people.

  • Mission

    To support government and private programs in accelerating the achievement of national health goals, particularly at the small and medium scale level, and to conduct regular training on the use and maintenance of medical equipment so that patient recovery can proceed without interruption.
