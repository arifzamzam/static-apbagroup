UI/UX Designer

Apbatech

HRD Division
Mar 15, 2025

APBATECH is a technology company under the APBA Group, focusing on web and mobile application development, digital branding, and IT infrastructure. We believe in building impactful digital products through collaboration, innovation, and user-centered design.

We are looking for a creative and detail-oriented UI/UX Designer to join our growing team. You will be responsible for turning ideas into intuitive and appealing digital experiences for clients across various industries.

Qualifications

Web Components have become increasingly crucial in modern web development, offering a standardized way to create reusable custom elements. Key advantages include:

  • Bachelor’s or Diploma degree in Design, Informatics, or a related field.
  • At least 1 year of experience in UI/UX Design (Fresh graduates with strong portfolios are welcome).
  • Solid understanding of UX principles and design systems.
  • Strong design portfolio demonstrating UI/UX work (web/mobile).

Requirements

  • Proficient in tools like Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, or similar.
  • Experience in wireframing, prototyping, and user flow creation.
  • Familiar with responsive and mobile-first design.
  • Basic understanding of HTML/CSS is a plus.
  • Strong communication and collaboration skills.
  • Ability to adapt quickly and manage multiple projects.
